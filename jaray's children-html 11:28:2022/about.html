About JARAY's Children

Jaray's Children is a website developed for Adelphi University's Computer Science Master Program. for the class of Software Engineering Methodology. None of the animals here are actually up for adoption or able to be purchased, therefore none of the animals have been harmed in the making of this website. Otherwise please enjpy our hard work and have an amazing day!
Please do not enter any sensitive information of yours into the site as we will not be responsible if that data is lost or stolen. The website itself was made as our group final project where we had to connect multiple users through a database. We have provided a few examples to our teacher Sung Kim on how each one will be able to interact with our database as well as the roles on what each person can do.
